Being an international freight forwarder, we can handle all types of cargo, irrespective of its nature, size, and volume. Marine Insurance

Insurance plays a vital role in an international marine time trade. One can't rely on liability cover provided by carrier as per various trade convention. It is highly recommended to cover insurance for all the cargo before it starts moving from the port of loading.

We, Time Rich, will extend our support and offer a best and competitive rate for Marine cargo, domestically moving freight by road or inland water. We partnered with some of the most acclaimed underwriters who can provide comprehensive cover for a wide variety of risk at an affordable rate.
